31663 Cuttlefish Robotics

FTC Team 31663 Cuttlefish Robotics from Calgary is a rookie team bringing innovation and teamwork to the 2025 season. Based at Renert School, they're diving into robotics with creativity and determination!

What We're Building Now

We're building our second-gen robot, the Cuddler UWU, designed for speed, agility, and smart automation. With the Limelight 3A for precise targeting and Pedropathing for smoother autonomous movement, it's faster and smarter than ever. Stay tuned for updates!

Our Current Stats

As of now we have places 3rd in Flint Meet 1 and 4th in the North Trail Scrimmage. Setting 3 provincial records and outperforming any other Calgary based teams in Auto. We are ranked in the top 129th percentile globally!

Tutorials And More

Our team, set towards educating and teaching rookies has created a RoboGuide for complete beginner to FTC. Going over the basics, from the begining steps of basic Cad Design to the fasinating world of code! Click Me!
